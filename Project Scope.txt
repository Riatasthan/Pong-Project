Outline:
  To make a simple game of pong that works on a keyboard
Scope:
  Make a game of pong using Javascript
  Two players will play each other
  Players will use the same keyboard
  Each player will control a paddle
  Player 1 will use 'W' and 'S' keys
  Player 2 will use 'Up' and 'Down' keys
  Will run on in a window
  Will have a white background
  Will have black paddles
  Will have a red ball
  Will have a ablitlity to pause a game
  Will have a score counter for Player 1 and Player 2
  Will have a rally counter
  Will have a win state when a player score >= 10
